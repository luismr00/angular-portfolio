About

Hi, my name is Luis, and I am a recent Computer Science graduate from California State University Northridge and also a veteran from the U.S. Navy. My service in the military consisted of working in patient care as the rate or title of a Hospital Corpsman (2012 - 2017). While performing my duties in the medical field, I later realized I wanted to work with computers instead. Changing jobs in the Navy to something computer related was rather difficult, so I decided to finish my service honorably and went back to school to pursue Computer Science once my contract ended.

Upon leaving the service, I decided to move to Los Angeles instead of my original hometown of Chicago to go to school. There, I first started attending school in Los Angeles City College and later transferred to CSUN to pursue my Bachelor’s Degree. It took my 5 years to finish my degree, but I managed to finish in the end and learned a lot during my time in college. Plus, I also managed to get summer internship in 2021 over with Snapchat. There I learned more about full stack and app development along creating other things such as lenses.

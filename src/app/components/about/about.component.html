About Me

Hello, I'm Luis, a recent Computer Science graduate from California State University Northridge and a U.S. Navy veteran. During my military service from 2012 to 2017, I worked in patient care as a Hospital Corpsman. However, I discovered my passion for computers and decided to transition into the field. Despite the challenges of changing career paths within the Navy, I chose to complete my service honorably and pursue a degree in Computer Science after my contract ended.

After leaving the military, I opted to move to Los Angeles instead of my hometown of Chicago to attend school. I initially enrolled at Los Angeles City College before transferring to CSUN to earn my Bachelor’s Degree. Although it took me five years to complete my degree, the journey was rewarding, and I gained invaluable knowledge during my time in college. Additionally, I had the opportunity to intern at Snapchat in 2021, where I delved into full-stack and app development, including creating lenses and exploring various aspects of software development.
